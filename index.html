About Us

Located in the bustling Aundh area of Pune, Juice Destination began its journey in 2009 as a health-focused joint. Over the years, we evolved in response to customer demand, expanding our offerings to include wholesome meals and combos. Our daily meals became an instant hit, and to date, we've proudly served over 250,000 meals!

To meet this growing demand, we established a 1,200 sq. ft. satellite kitchen in 2014. This facility, branded as The Meal House, doesn't just serve as the heart of our culinary operations—it also functions as a highly efficient cloud kitchen.

At The Meal House, we've perfected the art of catering to everyday dining needs with our signature North Indian Thalis (Meals), delivered in a Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) format. This ensures that customers can enjoy high-quality meals without the hassle of heading to a fine-dining restaurant. Our consistent quality and service have converted countless first-timers into loyal patrons, with 60% of our customer base returning regularly, rather daily!

We're a trusted favorite among corporates, proudly serving esteemed clients like Star Union Dai-ichi Life Insurance, State Bank of India, NTT Data, Velotio Technologies, UST, and many more. We cater to over 90% of corporate offices in the NSG IT Park, Aundh, while also being a popular choice among the local student community.

Our delivery radius spans 15 kilometers, ensuring fresh, delicious meals reach not just offices and students but also nearby residents and hospitals. Alongside our meal offerings, our refreshing juices and snacks have become a daily staple for many.

Whether you're craving a wholesome meal, a refreshing juice, or a quick snack, The Meal House is your one-stop solution for health, convenience, and flavor.
